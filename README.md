#  E-commerce Delivery Performance & Customer Experience Analysis

##  Project Overview
This project analyzes delivery performance in an e-commerce marketplace to understand how delivery delays impact customer satisfaction and revenue.

The analysis focuses on identifying key operational drivers such as seller handling time, geographic factors, and seller-level performance.

---

## Business Problem
Delivery delays negatively impact customer satisfaction and platform trust. Even a small percentage of delayed orders can lead to poor customer experience and revenue risk.

---

## Key Questions
- How frequently do delivery delays occur?
- How do delays impact customer review scores?
- Which sellers contribute most to delivery delays?
- Which regions experience the highest delays?
- How does seller handling time affect delivery performance?
- Which sellers contribute most to delayed revenue?

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- SQL (SQLite)
- Data Visualization (Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Jupyter Notebook

---

##  Key Insights
- ~6.7% of orders are delayed but significantly reduce customer ratings (4.29 → 2.27)
- Seller handling time is a major contributor to delivery delays
- High-volume sellers drive most delayed revenue
- Delivery delays vary significantly across regions

---

##  Business Recommendations
- Target high-impact sellers contributing to delayed revenue
- Improve logistics in high-delay regions
- Reduce seller handling time
- Implement seller performance monitoring

---

##  Outcome
This project demonstrates how data analysis can identify operational inefficiencies and provide actionable insights to improve delivery performance and customer experience.
